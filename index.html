This is a simple model of Earth's radiative balance. The model operates with an albedo and absorbance affecting the incoming solar radiation, and an emissivity describing the infrared properties of the atmosphere. You can also scale the intensity of the incoming solar radiation. The model calculates the global mean temperature of the surface and the atmosphere.
